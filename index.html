About me - Faris Ibrišević

Hello and welcome to my corner of the digital world! I'm Faris Ibrišević, a 21-year-old enthusiast with a fervent passion for a diverse range of fields. From the exciting realms of CAD design and 3D printing to the intricate worlds of network and system administration, Formula 1, IT, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity, I'm on an endless quest to explore and master the intricacies of these fascinating domains.

CAD and 3D Printing

Ever since I laid eyes on the possibilities of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and 3D printing, I've been captivated by the idea of turning imagination into tangible reality. The power to design and create three-dimensional objects from scratch is a driving force behind my creativity. I believe that every idea, no matter how abstract, can be visualized and brought to life through the magic of CAD and 3D printing.

System and Network administration

The world of technology is built upon interconnected networks and robust systems. With a keen eye for detail and a penchant for problem-solving, I've immersed myself in the realm of network and system administration. Ensuring smooth operations, security, and scalability in a digital landscape that's constantly evolving is a challenge I eagerly embrace.
